About AuraCheck AI

We built AuraCheck to make personality insight accessible, entertaining, and meaningful — combining the wisdom of aura reading with the power of modern artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

AuraCheck AI exists at the intersection of ancient wisdom and modern technology. Our mission is simple: give every person on the planet instant access to a personalized energy reading that's equal parts insightful and entertaining.

We believe that self-awareness is the foundation of a well-lived life. Whether you're using our Brutal Roast mode for a laugh with friends or our True Aura mode for genuine self-reflection, every reading is an invitation to look at yourself a little more honestly — and maybe share that with the people who matter to you.

The Story Behind AuraCheck

AuraCheck was founded in 2026 by a small team of developers and designers fascinated by the collision of internet culture, personality psychology, and AI capabilities. We noticed that "vibe checks" had become a genuine cultural phenomenon — people were using shorthand energy assessments to communicate personality fit, compatibility, and social trust.

But every existing tool was either a generic personality quiz or an overly serious spiritual platform. Nothing captured the playful, shareable, authentic way that the internet's Generation Z and millennial communities actually think about personality. So we built AuraCheck.

Starting with a simple roast generator, we quickly discovered something unexpected: users weren't just sharing their results for laughs. Many were using the True Aura readings for genuine self-reflection, saving their cards as digital snapshots of where they were in life at a particular moment, and even sharing readings with therapists and coaches as conversation starters.

That feedback reshaped everything. We invested deeply in the quality of our AI analysis, added the full aura color guide and personality frameworks you see on the site today, and committed to making AuraCheck a platform you can return to as you grow and change.
